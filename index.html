About MeruApp

Bringing Government Closer to the People

MeruApp is the official digital platform of the County Government of Meru, designed to bridge the gap between public institutions and residents. Whether you need to access county services, stay updated on development projects, or connect with local businesses, MeruApp puts it all at your fingertips.

Built with transparency, accessibility, and citizen empowerment at its core, MeruApp represents our commitment to digital governance and inclusive public service delivery for all residents of Meru County.
